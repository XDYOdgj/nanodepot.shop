Empava 36" Gas Cooktop

The Empava 36" Gas Cooktop is built with a convertible LPG/NG setup, allowing you to switch between fuels effortlessly. Whether you're cooking at home or on the go, this flexibility makes it a perfect fit for multiple settings.

Equipped with five high-performance SABAF burners, each has flame failure protection to ensure safe and consistent heat. This precise control lets you cook with confidence, perfectly adjusting heat for whatever your recipe requires.

Built to Last

Durable cast iron grates and reinforced knobs ensure ease of use and long-term reliability. These components are designed to withstand frequent use and can be easily cleaned in the dishwasher for hassle-free maintenance.

A Reliable Kitchen Companion

Whether you're preparing a quick meal or entertaining guests, the Empava 36" Gas Cooktop offers an efficient, reliable solution. Its user-friendly design and high-quality construction make it a standout choice for both home chefs and outdoor enthusiasts.
