Anlyter 30-Inch Built-In Dual Fuel Gas Cooktop

Elevate your cooking experience with the Anlyter 30-inch built-in dual fuel gas cooktop. This versatile appliance features a powerful 5-burner configuration, offering everything from high-heat searing to precise low-heat simmering. Whether you're whipping up a gourmet meal or mastering everyday recipes, this cooktop delivers exceptional performance and control.

Designed with premium stainless steel, the Anlyter cooktop is built to last and offers an easy-to-clean surface that complements your kitchen's aesthetic. Its user-friendly interface includes individual burner controls and a one-second ignition system, ensuring intuitive operation with minimal effort. Enjoy the convenience of automatic ignition and a pre-installed natural gas cord, making installation and setup quick and hassle-free.

The built-in wok support ring enhances functionality, providing added stability for larger cookware. This gas cooktop is ideal for both casual cooking and professional-grade performance, offering dual fuel compatibility for use with LPG or NG. With a sleek black finish, it blends seamlessly into any kitchen design, and comes with a 1-year warranty to give you confidence in your purchase.
