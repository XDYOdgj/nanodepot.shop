Perfect for boat building and repair, our Structural Polymer Putty delivers unmatched performance for bonding dissimilar composites such as fiberglass, aluminum, and wood. Whether you're working on hull-to-deck, stringer-to-hull, or liner-to-hull connections, this putty ensures lasting strength and durability for your marine projects.

  • Designed for both automotive and marine applications
  • Mixable and pressable for seamless application
  • Bonds a variety of dissimilar materials with ease
  • Cures to a surface tack, enabling efficient touch-ups and repairs
