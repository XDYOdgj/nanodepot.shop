Henshar 30 Inch Gas Cooktop

Elevate your cooking experience with the Henshar 30 inch gas cooktop. Designed for home chefs who value performance and versatility, it combines advanced functionality with a sleek, modern look. This cooktop features five sealed burners, giving you precise control over heat levels for a wide range of cooking techniques, from gentle simmering to high-heat stir-frying and caramelizing. The durable cast iron base ensures long-lasting stability and resilience, while the tempered glass surface is not only stylish but also resistant to scratches and corrosion, making it easy to clean and maintain.

Whether you're crafting a gourmet meal or preparing a simple family dinner, this Henshar gas cooktop adapts to your needs with ease. Its compact design fits seamlessly into most countertops, and the NG/LPG convertible feature gives you the flexibility to use your preferred fuel type. The pre-installed 110V power cord and regulator make setup a breeze, eliminating the need for additional installations and offering a hassle-free cooking experience that every homeowner can appreciate.
